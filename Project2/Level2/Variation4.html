The Rose Family


                The rose is a
                r    o    s     e,

                And was always a rose.


                                But the theory now goes

                                That the apple’s a
                                r      o       s        e,



                And the pear is, and so’s

                      The plum, I suppose.


                                The dear only knows

                                What will next prove a
                                r          o         s          e.




                You, of course, are a
                r         o        s         e  –

                


                                But were always a
                                r       o       s       e.



                Robert Frost
