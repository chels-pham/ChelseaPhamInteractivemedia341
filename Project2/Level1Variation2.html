             The Rose Family


                                 The rose
                                 is a rose,                      And was
                                 always a
                                 rose.


                                 But the
                                 theory now
                                 goes
                                 That the
                                 apple’s a
                                 rose,                                  And the
                                 pear is,
                                 and so’s
                                 The plum,
                                 I suppose.


                                 The dear
                                 only knows
                                 What will
                                 next prove
                                 a rose.


                                 You,
                                 of course,
                                 are a rose –                                  But were
                                 always a
                                 rose.


             Robert Frost
